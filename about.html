My Background

My name is Eva Domschot, and I am currently a Graduate student at New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology. The focus of my Master's degree is in Artificial Intelligence, and I have also completed a graduate certificate in Cybersecurity. The current work in my thesis focuses on machine learning techniques to help solve the most challenging Cybersecurity problems that we face. Currently, malware attacks are a looming threat to all of the vital infrastructure in this country. That is why I have spent the last several years focusing on new strategies in which malware can be targeted and classified.
